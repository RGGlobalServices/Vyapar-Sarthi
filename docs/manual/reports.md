# Reports

What your shop actually earned.

## What you get

- **Total Revenue** — everything you sold.
- **Total Profit** — what you kept after cost price.
- **Profit Margin** — profit as a percentage of revenue.
- **Sales vs Profit** — a chart over time, so you can see whether busy days are
  actually profitable ones.
- **Top Selling** — your best items.
- **Category Performance** — which kinds of goods earn most.

The default view is the **Last 7 Days**; you can change the period.

## Profit needs cost prices

Revenue is counted from your bills, so it's always right. **Profit is only as
good as your cost prices.** If a product has no cost price in
[Products](products.md), the app cannot know what you paid, and that item's
profit will look wrong — usually too high.

If your profit figures look too good to be true, check for products with
missing cost prices first.

## Exporting

**Export** downloads the report so you can keep it, print it, or send it to
your accountant. Export is available on all packages.

## Related screens

- **Dashboard** — the quick daily view when you open the app.
- **Calendar** — your business days and upcoming events, with a badge in the
  sidebar when something is due.
