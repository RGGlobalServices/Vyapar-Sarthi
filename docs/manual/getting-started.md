# Getting Started

## Signing in

Open the app and you land on the sign-in screen.

- **Email and password** — enter the details you registered with, then
  **Sign In**.
- **Continue with Google** — sign in with your Google account instead.
- **Forgot password?** — sends a reset link to your email.
- **Create Account** — if you don't have an account yet.

## Setting up your shop

When you first create a shop, you are asked for:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Shop / Store Name | Yes | Appears on your bills |
| Business Category | Yes | See below — this changes how the app behaves |
| Contact Number | No | Printed on bills |
| GST Number | No | Printed on bills if entered |
| Shop Address | No | Printed on bills |

You can change all of these later in [Settings & Profile](settings-and-profile.md).

### Business category

Pick the one closest to your shop. The app supports:

- Kirana & Grocery
- Pharmacy & Medical
- Apparel & Clothes
- Footwear & Shoes
- Electronics & Mobiles
- Hardware & Tools
- Cosmetics & Beauty
- General Wholesale

This is not just a label. It changes the units you get by default, whether
items have sizes or variants, and which extras appear — for example, an
Electronics shop sees EMI options in billing, and an Apparel shop gets size
variants on products.

Your category is shown as a badge at the top of the sidebar, next to your
package name.

## Finding your way around

The **sidebar** on the left is your main menu. At the top it shows your shop
name, your business category and your package. Below that are your sections —
Dashboard, Billing, Products, Stock and so on.

Which sections appear depends on your package. See
[Packages & Plans](packages.md) for the full list.

At the bottom of the sidebar:

- **Theme** — switch between light and dark.
- **Language** — **EN** (English), **HI** (हिंदी), **MR** (मराठी). The change
  applies immediately.
- **Access Role** — shows your role, for example ADMIN.
- **Logout** — asks you to confirm before signing out.

On a phone, the sidebar is hidden behind the menu button.

## Your first bill

1. Open **Billing**.
2. Search for a product by name, or type/scan its barcode.
3. Click the product to add it to the cart.
4. Choose how the customer is paying.
5. Click **Confirm Sale**, enter the customer's name if needed, and confirm.

Full details are in [Billing](billing.md).

## If you have more than one shop

On the Vyapar and Udyog packages you can run more than one shop from the same
account. Use the shop switcher near the top of the sidebar to move between
them. Each shop keeps its own products, stock, bills and udhar — nothing is
shared between shops.
