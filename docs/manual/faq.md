# FAQ & Troubleshooting

## Billing

**A section is missing from my sidebar.**
Your package decides what appears. Customers and Udhar are on Dukaan and
Vyapar; Orders, Party, Suppliers, Warehouses and Purchases are on Udyog. See
[Packages & Plans](packages.md).

**My barcode scanner isn't adding items.**
Click into the billing screen first so it has focus, then scan. The app reads
the scanner as fast typing, so it needs the page to be active. If it still
doesn't work, check the scanner types into a text box elsewhere — if it
doesn't, the problem is the scanner or its cable, not the app.

**The barcode scanned but the product wasn't found.**
That barcode isn't in your catalogue yet. The app offers to create the product
there and then — do that once and it'll be recognised from then on.

**I can't save an udhar bill.**
A customer name is required whenever any amount is outstanding. Enter a name in
the Customer Details window, or take full payment instead.

**I sold something I don't stock.**
Use **Manual Add** in billing. Remember it only affects that one bill — if
you'll sell it again, create a real product in [Products](products.md).

**The customer paid part now and part later.**
Choose **Udhar**, then enter the amount in **Pay Now** and pick how it was
received. The rest goes to their ledger automatically. See
[Billing](billing.md#udhar--pay-now-pay-later).

## Money and reports

**My profit looks wrong.**
Check your cost prices. Profit is selling price minus cost price, so any
product with a missing cost price will report inflated profit. See
[Reports](reports.md#profit-needs-cost-prices).

**My cash drawer doesn't match at day end.**
Check that payment methods were recorded truthfully — a UPI sale marked as
cash, or a UPI expense recorded as cash, will each throw the figure off by that
amount. This applies to udhar advances too: use **Received via** to say how the
money actually arrived.

**A customer disagrees about their balance.**
Open them in [Udhar](udhar-and-customers.md) and show their Transaction
History — every udhar given and payment received, with dates.

## Stock

**Stock numbers are wrong.**
Sales reduce stock automatically, so don't also record a Stock Out for them, or
it'll be deducted twice. For goods lost, damaged or expired, do use Stock Out.

**I stopped selling an item.**
Archive it rather than deleting. Archiving hides it from billing but keeps its
history in your reports. Deleting cannot be undone. See [Stock](stock.md).

## Account

**Can I use more than one shop?**
Yes on Vyapar (up to 3) and Udyog (unlimited). Each shop keeps its own
products, stock, bills and udhar — nothing is shared.

**My subscription expired. Did I lose my data?**
No. Your data is intact. You keep access to Billing, Products, Stock and
Settings so you can carry on trading and renew; other sections return once you
renew.

**How do I change the app's language?**
**Language** at the bottom of the sidebar — EN, HI or MR. It changes
immediately.

**How long is the free trial?**
7 days, or 14 days if you signed up with a referral code. Paid plans have a
30-day money-back guarantee.
