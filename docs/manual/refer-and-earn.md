# Refer & Earn

Invite other shopkeepers and get rewarded.

Refer & Earn is available on the **Vyapar** and **Udyog** packages.

## How it works

1. Open **Refer & Earn** to find **Your Code**.
2. Share it — **Copy Link** or **Share on WhatsApp**.
3. They sign up using your code.
4. You earn a reward when their subscription goes through.

## What you get

The screen tracks your **Total Referrals**, how many were **Successful**, and
the **Free Months** you've earned.

## What they get

Someone signing up with a referral code gets a **14-day free trial** instead of
the usual 7 days — so it's worth them using your code even before they decide
to pay.

## Team

If you refer several shopkeepers, they appear under **Team** so you can see who
joined through you.
