# Udhar & Customers

Your credit ledger — who owes you money, and how much.

Udhar is on the **Dukaan** and **Vyapar** packages. Udyog uses
**Party / Ledger** instead — see [Udyog (Wholesale)](udyog-wholesale.md).

## The udhar screen

The top shows your **Total Outstanding** — everything owed to you across all
customers. Below is your customer list with each person's **Total Due** and
**Last Activity**, so you can see who has owed you the longest.

If nobody owes you anything, the screen says so — everything is cleared.

## How udhar gets added

Most udhar comes from billing. When you choose **Udhar** as the payment method
on a bill, the outstanding amount is added to that customer's ledger
automatically, and the bill is linked to it. You don't need to record anything
here by hand.

If the customer pays part of the bill up front, only the balance is added. See
[Billing](billing.md#udhar--pay-now-pay-later).

You can also **Add Udhar** directly from this screen for money owed outside a
bill.

## Recording a repayment

When a customer pays back:

1. Open the customer.
2. Click **Record Payment**.
3. Enter the amount and an optional note.

Their total due drops by that amount. Partial repayments are fine — enter
whatever they actually paid.

## Customer history

Open any customer to see their **Transaction History**: every udhar given and
every payment received, oldest to newest. This is the record to show a customer
who disagrees about their balance.

## Adding and editing customers

**New Customer** asks for a name and mobile number. The mobile number is worth
filling in — it's how reminders reach them on WhatsApp.

You can edit a customer's name or number later.

New customers are also created automatically when you type a new name into an
udhar bill.

## Reminders

You can send udhar reminders on WhatsApp or by email. Turn these on in
[Settings & Profile](settings-and-profile.md).

## Limits

| Package | Udhar customers |
|---|---|
| Dukaan | Up to 100 |
| Vyapar | Unlimited |
| Udyog | Uses Party / Ledger instead |
