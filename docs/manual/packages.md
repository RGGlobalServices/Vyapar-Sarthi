# Packages & Plans

Vyapar Sarthi comes in three packages. Your package decides which sections
appear in your sidebar and what limits apply.

| Package | Price | Built for |
|---|---|---|
| **Dukaan** | ₹299/month | A single retail shop |
| **Vyapar** | ₹499/month | A growing business, up to 5 shops |
| **Udyog** | ₹999/month | Wholesale and distribution |

Your current package is shown as a badge at the top of the sidebar.

## What each package includes

| | Dukaan | Vyapar | Udyog |
|---|---|---|---|
| Products | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Udhar customers | Up to 100 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Shops | 1 | Up to 5 | Unlimited |
| Auto-send bill on WhatsApp/Email | — | Yes | Yes |
| Warehouses | — | — | Yes |
| Dukandar management | — | — | Yes |
| Refer & Earn | — | Yes | Yes |
| Staff management | — | Yes | Yes |

## Sections in the sidebar

**Dukaan and Vyapar** — Dashboard, Billing, Products, Customers, Stock,
Expenses, Udhar, Staff, Reports, Calendar, Returns, Refer & Earn, Dukandar,
Settings, Profile. Vyapar adds **Import Data**.

**Udyog** — Dashboard, Orders, Billing, Products, Party / Ledger, Suppliers,
Warehouses, Purchases, Stock, Stock Transfer, Expenses, Staff, Reports,
Import Data, Calendar, Returns, Refer & Earn, Dukandar, Settings, Profile.

Note that Udyog is a different shape of business, not simply "more":
it replaces **Customers** and **Udhar** with **Party / Ledger**, which handles
both customers and suppliers with credit terms. Billing also works
differently — see [Udyog (Wholesale)](udyog-wholesale.md).

## Free trial

New shops start on a **free trial**:

- **7 days** normally.
- **14 days** if you signed up with a referral code.

During the trial you have full access. There is also a **30-day money-back
guarantee** on paid plans.

## Renewing and upgrading

Each payment gives you **30 days** of validity. To subscribe, renew or
upgrade, open the payment screen from the sidebar or from
**Settings → Subscription**, choose your plan and pay.

Your subscription is in one of these states:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Trial** | Free trial running, full access |
| **Active** | Paid and current |
| **Expired** | Trial or plan has lapsed — limited access |
| **Cancelled** | You cancelled the subscription |

## When your subscription ends

The app does not lock you out completely. You can still reach the core of your
shop so you can keep trading and renew — including **Billing**, **Products**,
**Stock** and **Settings**. Other sections ask you to renew first.

To cancel, go to **Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription**. Your
billing history is available on the same screen.
