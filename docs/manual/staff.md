# Staff

Your workers — who they are, who turned up, and what they're owed.

Staff is available on the **Vyapar** and **Udyog** packages.

## The staff screen

The top shows **Total Staff**, **Present Today**, **Absent Today** and
**Pending Salary**. Below is your staff list with each person's contact,
status and salary.

## Adding staff

Click **Add Staff** and enter their name, contact number and salary.

## Attendance

Use **Attendance** to mark who is present or absent each day. The counts at
the top update as you mark people, and the attendance record is what backs up
salary calculations at month end.

## Salary

The **Pending Salary** figure shows what you still owe across your staff. Open
a staff member to see and update their salary details.

## Access roles

Your own role is shown at the bottom of the sidebar — for example **ADMIN**.
Roles control what a person can see and do in the app.
