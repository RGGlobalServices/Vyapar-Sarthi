# Returns

When a customer brings something back.

## Taking a return

1. Open **Returns**.
2. **Find Invoice** — enter the invoice ID from the customer's bill and search.
3. The **Invoice Summary** appears: customer, date, total and what was paid.
4. Under **Return Items**, select the items being returned.
5. Confirm the return.

Returning items puts them back into stock and adjusts the sale.

## Finding the invoice number

It's printed on the bill slip, in the form `INV-XXXXXXXX`. If the customer
doesn't have their bill, look the sale up under **Invoices** in
[Billing](billing.md#finding-old-bills).

## Guest bills

Bills made without a customer name show as **Guest**. They can still be
returned — you just need the invoice number.

## Returns on an udhar bill

If the original bill was on udhar, returning items reduces what the customer
owes. Check their balance in [Udhar](udhar-and-customers.md) afterwards to
confirm it looks right.
