# Settings & Profile

## Profile — your shop details

Your shop name, business category, contact number, GST number and address.
These are what appear on your bills, so keep them correct.

You can change your business category later, but it affects how the app
behaves — units, variants and extras like EMI. Change it only if you genuinely
sell something different now.

## Bill appearance

Set how your bills look:

- **Invoice format** — thermal (for a small receipt printer) or A4.
- **GST / PAN** — printed on the bill when filled in.
- **QR code** — show a QR code on the bill.
- **Invoice footer** — your own message at the bottom, such as thanks or a
  returns policy.

## Notifications

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Push notifications** | Turn alerts on for this device |
| **Daily summary** | A morning message with yesterday's profit |
| **Low stock alert** | Tells you what's running out |
| **Alert time** | When the daily alerts arrive |

You can also turn on **udhar reminders** by WhatsApp or email, so customers get
nudged about what they owe without you having to ask.

Notifications are per device. If you use the app on your phone and your
counter machine, turn them on where you actually want to be told.

## Subscription

Shows your current plan and lets you:

- **Upgrade or renew** — each payment gives 30 days.
- **See billing history** — your past payments.
- **Cancel subscription** — stops future renewals.

There's a **30-day money-back guarantee** on paid plans. See
[Packages & Plans](packages.md).

## Language and theme

At the bottom of the sidebar: **Theme** for light/dark, and **Language** for
**EN** / **HI** / **MR**. Both apply immediately.

## Account

**Logout** is at the bottom of the sidebar and asks you to confirm.

If you run more than one shop (Vyapar and Udyog), use the shop switcher at the
top of the sidebar. Each shop has its own settings — setting your invoice
footer on one shop does not change the others.
